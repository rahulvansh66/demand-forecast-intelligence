Aim is to develop two models:

1. Demand Forecasting Model

Problem Statement: Predict daily product sales for the next 28 days.

2. Product Sales Segmentation Model

Problem Statement: Cluster products into 5 segment based on sales behavior.

Segment 1: High Demand, Stable Products
Segment 2: High Demand, Volatile Products
Segment 3: Low Demand, Intermittent Products
Segment 4: Declining Products
Segment 5: Seasonal Products
